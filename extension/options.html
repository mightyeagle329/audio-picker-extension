I don't know). Otherwise enumerateDevices() returns empty device labels which makes it rather hard to actually pick the desired device.

Note that calls to getUserMedia() do not work when the Options page is a popup window on the Extensions tab, i. e. it's defined via options_ui in the manifest (which it is). In that case the devices will show up with their internal ids and not with their human readable labels and you will need to do the following, once.

  • Open the Options Full Page
  • Approve the one-time request to access your local media (microphone (and camera))
    Your (previous) approval is indicated by a red dot on the tab
  • Refresh the options full page (AudioPick Options tab) by pressing F5
  • Close the options full page (AudioPick Options tab)
  • Refresh the options_ui popup window (Extensions tab) by pressing F5
  • Procede to ...
